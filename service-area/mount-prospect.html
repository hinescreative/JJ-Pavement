Mount Prospect, IL

Asphalt & Concrete Contractor in Mount Prospect, IL

Mount Prospect borders Arlington Heights to the east and south, making it one of the closest communities to our home office. With over 54,000 residents, Mount Prospect is a well-established residential suburb with a walkable downtown along Northwest Highway and quiet, tree-lined neighborhoods throughout. J&J Pavement Repairs has been paving driveways and pouring concrete for Mount Prospect homeowners since 1982 — many of our earliest customers were right here. We're familiar with the village's permit requirements and work regularly with local property managers and HOAs.
