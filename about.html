Our Story

A family business
built on honesty.

J&J Pavement Repairs was founded in 1982 by John Pancorvo Sr. — built on a simple idea: do quality work, treat people honestly, and the reputation takes care of itself. He started with a small crew and a commitment to doing the job right the first time.

Forty years later, his son John Pancorvo Jr. carries that same commitment forward. The company is still family-run, still headquartered in Arlington Heights, and still serving many of the same communities where it started — now with a perfect 5-star Google rating and an A+ BBB rating with zero complaints to show for it.

We believe quality work is an investment. Our customers return to us time and again because pavement done right the first time lasts for decades — and that peace of mind is worth every penny.

Proud Partner of Folds of Honor

Supporting the families of America's fallen

A portion of every J&J project goes directly to Folds of Honor — a nonprofit providing educational scholarships to the spouses and children of fallen or disabled U.S. service members. When you hire J&J, you're supporting a business that gives back to those who gave everything.
