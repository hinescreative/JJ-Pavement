Sealcoating

Protect your investment.
Extend your pavement's life.

Professional sealcoating for residential driveways and commercial parking lots. The most cost-effective way to protect your asphalt.

Why Sealcoating Matters

Your asphalt's best defense against the elements.

Asphalt is tough, but it's not invincible. Sun, rain, snow, salt, and daily traffic wear it down over time. Sealcoating adds a protective layer that shields the surface from UV damage, prevents water from seeping into cracks, and keeps your pavement looking fresh.

Think of it like a coat of paint for your asphalt — except it's engineered to protect and extend the life of your surface by years. Applied every 2–3 years, sealcoating is the most cost-effective maintenance you can do.

We sealcoat residential driveways and commercial parking lots throughout the NW suburbs. Small cracks are filled before sealing. The result is a clean, uniform surface that looks new and resists the damage that leads to expensive repairs.

Benefits

Why sealcoat your asphalt?

Prevents Cracking

Seals small surface cracks before they grow into expensive structural problems.

Blocks UV Damage

Protects asphalt binder from sun oxidation — the #1 cause of surface deterioration.

Seals Water Out

Prevents water penetration that causes freeze-thaw damage and base erosion.

Restores Appearance

Gives faded, gray asphalt a fresh, deep-black finish that boosts curb appeal.
